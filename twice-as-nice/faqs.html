FAQS

SAME DAY DELIVERIES, PLEASE?

While we aim to fulfill all your cookie cravings, we need at least a one-day lead time to bake them to perfection. On special occasions, we do offer same-day deliveries, so be sure to follow our Instagram page for updates. If your cravings are particularly strong, feel free to message us, and we might just make an exception for you!

MODE OF PAYMENT?

Gcash, Paypal, or COD!

WHEN CAN I GET MY COOKIES?

Typically, you can expect to receive your cookies within 5 hours of placing your order. For same-day deliveries, we strive to have your freshly baked cookies delivered by 6 PM If you need your cookies at a specific time, please let us know, and we will do our best to accommodate your request!

HOW LONG CAN I KEEP MY COOKIES?

Our cookies are best enjoyed fresh, but they typically have a shelf life of:

  • Room Temperature: 1 week when stored in an airtight
    • container.
  • Refrigerated: Up to 2 weeks.

BEST WAY TO ENJOY MY COOKIES?

For the ultimate cookie experience, we recommend:
  • Fresh Out of the Oven: Enjoy them warm, right out of the packaging, if possible.
  • Pair with Milk: A classic combination that never fails.
  • Microwave: Warm them up in the microwave for 10-15 seconds to recreate that fresh-baked taste.
  • Ice Cream Sandwich: Use our cookies to make delicious ice cream sandwiches.
